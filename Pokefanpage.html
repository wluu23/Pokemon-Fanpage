The History of Pokemon

Pokemon is a media franchise created by Satoshi Tajiri in 1995. Many of us probably grew up with these creatures, as we have been exposed to them in many ways. The basis of the story is that humans, called Pokemon Trainers, catch and train Pokemon creatures in order to have them battle each other

Pokemon started off as video games for the Game Boy console, but its popularity allowed it to expand to trading cards, television shows and movies. Most recently, there was a huge fad over the smartphone app, Pokemon Go, which allowed people to act as Pokemon Trainers, using their very own cellular device. If you haven't experienced Pokemon yet, give it a shot! Warning: The games can get very addicting.
